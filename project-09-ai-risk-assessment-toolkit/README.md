# AI Risk Assessment Toolkit

**Project 9 of the AI Governance Portfolio**
**Author:** Karun. · AIGP (AI Governance Professional)

## Disclaimer

This project is a portfolio artefact created for demonstration and educational purposes only. It does not represent the policies, systems, or practices of any real organisation. Nothing in this toolkit constitutes legal advice.

## What This Is

A structured toolkit for identifying, assessing, treating, and monitoring AI-specific risk across an organisation — methodology through to a live monitoring dashboard.

## Contents

| # | Artefact | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | AI Risk Assessment Methodology | md | Risk categories, likelihood/impact definitions, treatment strategies, approval authority |
| 2 | AI Risk Register Template | xlsx | Working register with auto-calculated score and rating per risk |
| 3 | Risk Scoring Matrix | xlsx | Visual 5×5 likelihood × impact heat map with rating legend |
| 4 | Risk Treatment Plan Template | md | Per-risk treatment detail, by strategy (mitigate/transfer/avoid/accept) |
| 5 | Vendor AI Risk Assessment | md | Vendor-specific questionnaire and scoring |
| 6 | AI Incident Log Template | xlsx | Incident tracking with severity definitions sheet |
| 7 | Risk Monitoring Dashboard (Excel) | xlsx | Live KPIs, rating distribution pie chart, category breakdown bar chart |
| 8 | Risk Communication Template | md | Structured formats for Committee, operational, and external risk communication |

## How to Use

1. Read the Methodology first — it defines the scoring scale and treatment strategies everything else depends on.
2. Log every identified risk in the Risk Register; use the Scoring Matrix as the visual reference for likelihood/impact definitions.
3. For any risk requiring active treatment, complete a Risk Treatment Plan.
4. Vendor-introduced risk gets its own assessment via the Vendor AI Risk Assessment before onboarding.
5. Every confirmed incident goes in the Incident Log — this feeds the Dashboard automatically.
6. Use the Risk Communication Template to brief the right audience at the right level of detail.
