## Purpose

Once a system is confirmed High-Risk via the Risk Classification Worksheet (EUAI-002), use this checklist to track completion of every Article 8–17 obligation before production deployment. Nothing on this list is optional for a High-Risk system — items marked "N/A" must have a documented justification, not a blank.
