## Purpose

Confirms a High-Risk AI system is ready for its conformity assessment (Article 43) and supports drawing up the EU declaration of conformity (Article 47, Annex V) and CE marking.

## Step 1 — Determine the Conformity Assessment Route

| Question | Response |
|---|---|
| Is the system a stand-alone Annex III high-risk system (not embedded in a regulated product)? | Yes / No |
| Has the provider applied harmonised standards or common specifications in full? | Yes / No |

- If the system is a stand-alone Annex III system and harmonised standards were applied in full → **internal control** conformity assessment procedure (Annex VI) generally applies.
- If harmonised standards were not applied, or the system is a biometric identification system, or is embedded in a product already subject to third-party assessment under other EU law → a **notified body** assessment (Annex VII) may be required. Confirm with Legal before proceeding.
