# EU AI Act Compliance Toolkit

**Project 8 of the AI Governance Portfolio**
**Author:** Anju K. · AIGP (AI Governance Professional)

## Disclaimer

This project is a portfolio artefact created for demonstration and educational purposes only. It does not represent the policies, systems, or practices of any real organisation. Nothing in this toolkit constitutes legal advice — it should not be relied upon for actual regulatory compliance without review by qualified counsel.

## What This Is

Everything needed to assess and demonstrate compliance with the EU AI Act — from initial applicability through risk classification to conformity documentation and incident reporting.

## Contents

| # | Artefact | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | EU AI Act Applicability Assessment | md | Confirms the Act applies, and in which role(s) |
| 2 | Risk Classification Worksheet | xlsx | Structured tier determination with auto-calculated logic + Annex III reference sheet |
| 3 | High-Risk AI System Checklist | md | Article 8–17 pre-deployment checklist |
| 4 | Technical Documentation Template | md | Article 11 / Annex IV structure |
| 5 | Conformity Assessment Checklist | md | Route determination, declaration of conformity, CE marking |
| 6 | Human Oversight Procedures | md | Article 14 operationalised per-system |
| 7 | Post-Market Monitoring Plan | md | Article 72 monitoring cadence and thresholds |
| 8 | Incident Reporting Procedure | md | Article 73 serious incident definition, timelines, and internal escalation |

## How to Use

1. Start with the Applicability Assessment — confirm the Act applies and identify your role(s).
2. Run every system through the Risk Classification Worksheet.
3. For every High-Risk result, work through items 3–8 in order.
4. Human Oversight Procedures and Post-Market Monitoring Plan are completed per-system and stay live documents, updated as the system evolves.
