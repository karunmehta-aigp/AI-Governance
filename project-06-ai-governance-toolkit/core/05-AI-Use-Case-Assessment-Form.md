## Section D — Data Governance

- Data sources used for training/fine-tuning or retrieval, and their lawful basis.
- Whether personal data is processed, and if a DPIA or FRIA is required or already completed.
- Data retention period and deletion process.
- Known data quality or bias considerations (e.g. representativeness of training data for the affected population).

## Section E — Human Oversight

- Named individual empowered to override, pause, or stop the system.
- Point at which human review occurs (pre-decision, post-decision, sampling-based).
- Process for an affected individual to request human review or challenge an output.
