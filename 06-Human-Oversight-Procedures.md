## Purpose

Operationalises Article 14 of the EU AI Act for a specific High-Risk system: who oversees it, at what point, with what authority, and how that oversight is evidenced.

## 1. System Reference

| Field | Response |
|---|---|
| System ID | ORG-AI-0__ |
| System name | |
| Oversight model | Human-in-the-loop / Human-on-the-loop / Human-in-command |

**Model definitions:**
- **Human-in-the-loop** — a human reviews and approves every output before it takes effect.
- **Human-on-the-loop** — the system operates autonomously with a human monitoring and able to intervene in real time.
- **Human-in-command** — a human retains overall authority to decide whether/when to use the system and can override or deactivate it, without reviewing every individual output.
