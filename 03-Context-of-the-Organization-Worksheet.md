## Purpose

Satisfies ISO/IEC 42001 Clause 4 (4.1–4.4) — establishes the internal and external context, interested parties, and scope of the AI Management System before any policy or risk work begins. This is normally the first document completed in an ISO 42001 implementation.

## 2. Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties (Clause 4.2)

| Interested Party | Needs and Expectations | Relevant to AIMS? |
|---|---|---|
| Customers / end users | Trustworthy, safe, non-discriminatory AI outputs | Yes |
| Regulators | Legal compliance, ability to demonstrate conformity | Yes |
| Employees | Clear guidance on acceptable AI use, job impact transparency | Yes |
| Investors / board | Risk exposure visibility, reputational protection | Yes |
| Vendors / AI suppliers | Clear requirements, contractual expectations | Yes |
| Affected individuals (non-customers) | Fair treatment by automated decisions, right to explanation | Yes |
